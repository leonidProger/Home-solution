ABOUT US

Home Solutions is a group of initiative people - experts in the field of interior design.

The basic direction of activity of our company - creation of interior design, repair and construction works, production of furniture according to individual projects.

For us design is not just a job but, without exaggeration, a part of life. We keep an eye on all the new trends in world design, test new materials and technologies, and then implement the most exciting and effective innovations. At the same time, we strive to preserve and refine our own, recognizable style. In our activities, we rely not only on technology and innovation, but also promote the philosophy of proven human values.

Home Solutions is a practicality and functionality based on the use of available materials, because true aesthetics is always hidden in the imaginary simplicity, which in modern design is a huge work of experts and a number of high-tech developments.
